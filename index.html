How To Buy

Create a wallet

Download and install a friendly wallet like trust wallet or metamask on your browser, or if on mobile from the app store or google play store

GET SOME BNB

$PUP lives on the Smart Chain Blockchain so you need BNB (BSC) to purchase tokens. You can purchase BNB in most wallets, or deposit from an exchange.

GO TO PANCAKESWAP

Visit Pancakeswap to switch your $BNB to $PUP, set the slippage at 8% your purchase will be made

SWAP $BNB FOR $PUP.

Exchange your $BNB for $PUP on Trustwallet or Metamask, or other similar wallets. If you are having trouble, head over to our Telegram for assistance.
